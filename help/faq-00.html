JBroFuzz Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)   

Q: What can I find within this section?

 

Frequently asked questions cover basic guidelines and pointers towards aspects of using JBroFuzz, it's purpose and underlying philosophy.  

 

This section is structured into a number of sub-sections that can be accessed by clicking on the panel on the left hand side of this window.

 

More detailed information, can be found in the Help Topics section.

Q: Fancy wording what does JBroFuzz actually do?

 

JBroFuzz has the ability to send intentionally malformed data to any web server, recording the responses.

 

This yields testing certain apsects of the security of web servers, by crafting corresponding requests and reviewing the responses received.

 

The term stateless simply implies that the different requests/replies being generated do not depend on previous ones made.

Q: So what is the motto?

 

If you can't fuzz with JBroFuzz you probably don't want to fuzz!

 

Paraphrasing Chet's ending line speech, this application tries to make fuzzing as simple and less time consuming as possible.

Q: What JBroFuzz is not claiming to be...

 

The automated answer to your problems regarding sending a bunch of AAA's to a listening deamon service.

 

Unfortunately, you still have to know a bit about fuzzing web applications, HTTP and HTTPS, headers GET and POST statements, etc.

 

Q: Just what is it that you want to do?

 

Fuzzing, if done in the right way, can produce extremely successful results.

 

What is the key in this often mis-understood process, relates to what you are trying to achieve while selecting the payloads and categories of fuzzers that you will use.

 

Try to set a number of goals before you engage with any fuzzing exercise, in terms of what you are trying to do, how and in what time-span.

 

You cannot fuzz the entire universe!
